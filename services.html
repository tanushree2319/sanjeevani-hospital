Our Medical Services

Comprehensive and compassionate healthcare services available at Sanjeevani Hospital, Nagpur.

Emergency & Accident Care

Sanjeevani Hospital provides emergency and accident care services for patients requiring immediate medical attention. Our experienced team ensures timely diagnosis, stabilization, and treatment for critical conditions.

Medical Specialties

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of common and chronic illnesses including infections, hypertension, and lifestyle diseases.

Cardiology & Diabetes Care

Expert care for heart-related conditions and diabetes management including preventive consultation and treatment.

Orthopedic Care

Treatment for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal problems including fractures, arthritis, and injuries.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents including routine checkups and vaccinations.

ENT Care

Diagnosis and treatment for ear, nose, and throat conditions including infections, sinus issues, and related disorders.

Gynecology & Maternity Care

Our maternity and gynecology department provides complete care for women including pregnancy care, delivery services, reproductive health consultation, and women’s wellness treatment. The hospital is also a government registered MTP centre.

Surgical Services

Sanjeevani Hospital offers surgical treatment for a variety of medical conditions with the support of experienced surgeons and anesthesiologists. Our surgical services include general surgery, ENT surgery, and specialized procedures when required.
